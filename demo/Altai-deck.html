Problem

The smartest AI agent at a hedge fund
isn't allowed to open a web page.

  • Every desk is standing up internal AI agents for research. The CISO seals them: no outbound internet. That is the correct security posture.
  • But the signal that moves a stock lives outside the wall: alternative data, filings, supply-chain and hiring shifts, sentiment, and breach exposure. Exactly where the agent can't go.
  • Today's workaround is a human analyst who spends 42% of the time just gathering data, not analyzing (AFP/APQC), and is still never first.
  • First mover takes the trade. Everyone else reads the press release.
